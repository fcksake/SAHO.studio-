Warner Bros.

Consumer Product Development and Brand Assurance Internship
Los Angeles, Summer 2017



I had the pleasure to work with the creative team at Warner Bros. Consumer Product. The department dealt primarily with licensees on toys, fashion, home decor and publishing inspired by franchises such as the DC Cinematic Universe, Harry Potter, and Looney Tunes.

This opportunity provided me with a wide variety of both long and short term projects. This included franchise packaging concepts, ride features in Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi and concept sketches for upcoming superhero movies.

Some of projects I worked on are still in production and under NDA (Aquaman and Warner Brothers World). If you want to know more about my work at WB, contact me at saho@cmu.edu
